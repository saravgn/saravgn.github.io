PRADA fashion label PRODUCTIONS

Prada S.p.A. is an Italian fashion label specializing in luxury goods for men and women (ready-to-wear, leather and fashion accessories, shoes, luggage. The company was founded in 1913 by Mario Prada and it has become one of the most famous societies in the word thank's to it's original design an creativity. Additionally fashion has often dictated many design elements, such as whether shoes have very high heels or flat ones SUCH AS: PRADA’S HIGH-HEELED SHOES AND PRADA’S BALLERINES

PRADA'S HIGH-HEELED SHOES A shoe is an item of footwear intended to protect and comfort the human foot while doing various activities. Shoes are also used as an item of decoration. The design of shoes has varied enormously through time and from culture to culture, with appearance originally being tied to function.

PRADA’S BALLERINAS These ballerina flats are the essential accessory for any girl on the go who constantly needs to swap her heels for a comfy alternative. Whether it’s to console your feet after a long night of partying or simply a shopping trip you can’t face in heels the Butterfly Twists ballerina shoes are the perfect comfortable compliment. The ballerina flats are brilliant for driving around town safely, commuting to work in style and traveling in complete comfort these stylish multi-purpose ballerina shoes are ideal no matter what the occasion!
